Why we built this

didn’t want to just trust our SEO Agency blindly.
We wanted a way to help us keep track of the work our SEO Agency was doing
We didn’t have time to learn SEO just to monitor our SEO Agency.

We wanted to track the keywords that are important to us.
Our SEO Agency would provide us monthly keyword reports but what happens when we change SEO Agencie ?
We didn’t want to lose the ability to track our keywords if we changed SEO Agencies.

Many times we considered changing SEO Agencies, but we were worried our rankings would drop.
We needed a way to either be confident in our SEO Agency or confident about hiring a new one.
